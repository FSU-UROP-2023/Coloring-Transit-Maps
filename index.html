An easier way to understand subway line systems

Subways maps have been considered to be a useful tool in navigating transportation in the everyday life of a commuter. However, there seems to be a deficiency in current subways maps not taking into account color blind individuals, making it inaccessible for them to easily read subway systems.We expect this research approach to improve the quality of virtual subways maps, making them easier to read and more accessible to use for individuals with color vision deficiency.
